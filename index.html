Books n Vibes

Curated reviews of my all-time favorites

Dune

Frank Herbert's masterpiece weaves politics, religion, and ecology into an epic saga on the desert planet Arrakis. It's a richly imagined world that rewards patient readers with deep themes and unforgettable characters.

Dune Imperium

Set in the Dune universe, this strategy board game combines deck-building with worker placement. It captures the intrigue and combat of the books in a fast-paced tabletop experience that's perfect for game nights.

Children of Dune

The third book in Herbert's series delves into the lives of Paul Atreides' heirs as they grapple with prophecy and power. It's a thoughtful, sometimes haunting tale about legacy and destiny.

The Harry Potter Series
