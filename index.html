Our Story

Greedy Tamales was born from a simple idea: what if tamales, a dish with a rich and storied history, were elevated to a culinary art form? We set out to create the most decadent, flavorful, and unforgettable tamales in Seattle. We use only the finest, ethically sourced ingredients, and our recipes are a blend of time-honored tradition and modern culinary innovation. We know our tamales are a splurge, but we believe that some things in life are worth being greedy for.
